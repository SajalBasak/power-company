Company History

ZFLY Power Generation Co., Ltd. is a professional manufacturer of power generation equipment integrating R&D, production and sales. It has over 20 years of legacy with a registered capital of 50.6 million Yuan and is located at Fuan City, Fujian Province, China, covering an area of 10,000 square meters. ZFLY Power Generation Co., Ltd. has advanced production equipment, including laser cutting, sheet metal stamping, metalworking, winding and inlaying, plastic spraying, painting and assembly lines. With perfect quality control system and advanced series of testing equipment, we adopt scientific modern management mode and practice innovation of technology, production, quality and service with ISO9001 international quality management system as guarantee.
